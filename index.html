About DearPDF

DearPDF was born from a simple idea: PDF tools should be accessible, free, and secure for everyone. In today's digital world, PDFs are essential for business, education, and personal use. However, managing them often requires expensive, bulky software. We decided to change that.

Why Choose Us? Privacy First.

Our biggest commitment is to your privacy. Unlike other online tools, **we do not upload your files to a server**. All processing happens directly in your web browser using powerful JavaScript libraries. Your files never leave your computer, giving you complete peace of mind.

Our Core Tools

  • Merge & Split: Easily combine multiple PDFs into one or extract specific pages to create a new file.
  • Compress: Reduce the file size of your PDFs for easy sharing and storage, without significant quality loss.
  • Convert: Transform PDFs into other formats like images, and convert documents like Word, PowerPoint, and Excel into high-quality PDFs.
  • Edit & Sign: Make quick edits, add text, shapes, or draw your signature directly onto a PDF document.
  • Security: Add watermarks or rotate pages to prepare your documents for distribution.
